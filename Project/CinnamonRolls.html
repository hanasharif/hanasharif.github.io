Cinnamon Rolls

A very delicious and easy way of making cinnamon rolls.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 1 and 1/4 cup of Milk
  • 3 cups of All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of Sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Salt

For the Cinnamon Filling:

  • 1 cup of Caster Sugar
  • 2 tablespoon of Cinnamon Powder
  • 1 tablespoon of Instant Coffee Powder
  • 100 grams of butter, softened to room temperature

Preparation Method of the Filling:

Step 1: In a bowl combine the Softened Butter, Sugar, Cinnamon Powder and Instand Coffee. Then set aside until dough is ready to be filled.

Preparation Method of the Dough:

Step 2: In a bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar and salt.

Step 3: Then, add the Milk and Oil and knead either using a dough hooker of the stand mixer for 8 minutes on medium speed or by hand for 15 minutes.

Step 4: Then, if you have used a stand mixer, on a surface that has been dusted with flour knead the dough for another 5 minutes as demonstarted. If you are kneading using your hand from the very first, continue kneading for another 5-7 minutes.

Step 5: Now, put the dough in a bowl that has been greased with a bit of oil and cover it with cling film. Place it in a dark area or cover it with a towel allowing it to rest for about 45 minutes.

Step 6: Dust your working surface with a good quantity of flour.

Step 7: Place your rested dough on your working surface and using a rlling pin roll it out open into a rectangular shape.

Step 8: Now, spread the prepared filling on to the dough as demonsrated.

Step 9: Using your palm and bottom part of yor fingers roll the dough back as demonstrated.

Step 10: Using a thread divide th roll making each about 2 centimeters thick.

Step 11: Place the rolls in a oven safe pyrex and let it rest again for another 15 minutes.

Step 12: Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 170°C top and buttom.

Step 13: Now, place the pyrex in the oven in the middle lining and let bake until the top and bottom of the rolls are golden brown for about 20 minutes.

Bon Appétit!
