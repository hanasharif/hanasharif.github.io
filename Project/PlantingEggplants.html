Planting Eggplants

Eggplant is a plant specie from the nightshade family.

Tips on planting Eggplants:

  • You need to choose a sunny spot for eggplants.
  • You need to water them quite often.

The most common diseases(pests) that might affect Eggplants are:

  • Flea beetles
  • Leaf Miners
  • Powdery Mildews

To avoid getting your Eggplants in the risk of catching any pests, avoid watering the leafs and let them covered until they are strong to tolerate pests.

Neem Oil is also very much recommeneded once a week to control pests.
