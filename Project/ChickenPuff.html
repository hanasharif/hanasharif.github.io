Chicken Puff

A very delicious appetizer that can be served along with any cuisine as it'll be liked by both children and adults.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 900 grams of Chicken Breasts, cut into medium chunks
  • 3 medium Onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Black Pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of powdered Saffaron

For the Filling:

  • 5 tablespoons of Oil
  • 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 small can of Sweet Corn
  • 1 pack of fresh Mushroom, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of Curry Powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Poultry Spice
  • 1/2 cup of Pasta Sauce

For assembling the Puffs:

  • 1/2 cup of shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 Egg + 1/4 teaspoon of Vanilla
  • Puff Pastry

Preparation Method of the Chicken:

Step 1: In a non-stick pan, line the onions.

Step 2: Line the chicken on top of the onions.

Step 3: Add the salt and black pepper.

Step 4: Add the powdered Saffaron.

Step 5: Add the lemon juice all over the chicken.

Step 6: Cover the pan with aluminium foil and let it on low heat for 40 minutes.

Step 7: When Chicken is fully cooked, allow it to cool.

Preparation Method of the Filling:

Step 8: First, shred the Chicken.

Step 9: In a sauce pan, heat the oil and add in the garlic.

Step 10: When garlic odour is sensed, add in the shredded chicken breast and stir.

Step 11: Add the vegetables to the Chicken and mix until vegetables are equally spread.

Step 12: Then, add in the spices and mix well.

Step 13: Finally, add in the pasta sauce and then gently stir.

Step 14: Let it cool, and then assemble the puffs.

To Assemble the Puffs:

Step 15: Add about 1 tablespoon of the filling in the center of the puff and top with about 1 teaspoon of Mozzarella Cheese.

Step 16: Fold the pastry from one corner to the other in a diagonal manner to form a triamgular shape. Using a fork press the sides to make sure that the sides are tightened and closed well.

Step 17: Heat the oven from top and bottom on 170°C.

Step 18: Line your baking tray withh baking parchement.

Step 19: Mix the egg and vanilla to create the luminating brush of the puff.

Step 20: Line the puffs on the baking parchement keeping about 3 centimeters between each. Brush the top of the puffs with the luminating brush mix.

Step 21: Place the tray in the middle oven lining for about 12 minutes or until golden brown.

Bon Appétit!
