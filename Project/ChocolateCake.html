Chocolate Cake

A very light and fluffy chocolate cake.

Ingredients

For the Cake batter:

  • 500 grams of Chocolate Cake flour
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1/3 cup of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 cup + 2 tablespoons of Milk

For the Cake decoration:

  • 1 sachet of Cream Chantilly
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup of cold Milk
  • 1/2 cup of raw dark chocolate

Preparation Method of the Cake batter:

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 170°C assisted with the fan (Optional).

Step 2: Grease a 9 inch round springform cake pan with butter and cocoa powder (Optional).

Step 3: In a bowl, using an electrical whisk on low speed, combine the cake flour, eggs, vegetable oil and milk until smooth.

Step 4: After cake batter is smooth, beat the batter for 3 minutes on medium-high speed.

Step 5: Pour the batter in the greased pan and then, hit the pan twice on a hard surface just to get rid of excess air.

Step 6: Bake the cake in the preheated oven for 35-45 minutes depending on the power of your oven or until toothpick is placed in the center and is seen to be clean on the middle lining.

Step 7: When the cake is fully baked, let it cool for 20 minutes.

Step 8: Now, remove the cake from the pan and allow it to cool down completely.

Preparation method of the Cream for decorating the Cake:

Step 9: In a bowl combine the cream chantilly and vanilla essence.

Step 10: Add the milk, and whisk for 1 minute on low speed.

Step 11: Using the electrical whisk, on high speed, beat the cream chantilly mixture until thickened.

Step 12: Place the cream formed in a piping bag and then pipe it on the cake as demonstrated.

To assemble the Cake:

Step 13: In a microwave, melt the raw chocolate while mixing it between every 15 seconds intervals as demonstarted.

Step 14: Place the melted chocolate in a piping bag and pipe it on the serving plate as you like and Bon Appétit!
