Jeeb AlTajer

An authentic Arabic appetizer that is crusty and crunchy from the outside and filled with a very special, tender and juicy filling.

The name of this appetizer is translated as the pocket of the wealthy man due to how it is being served. It is served as a a crispy bread filled with the mouth-watering filling.

Ingredients

For the pockets:

  • 1 pack of White sliced bread
  • 4 Eggs + 2 teaspoons of Black Pepper + 1 teaspoon of Salt + 2 teaspoons of Mexican Spice
  • 1 and 1/2 cup of Bread Crumbs or All purpose flour + 1 tablespoon of Mexican Spice + 1 tablespoon of Italian Seasoning + 1/2 tablespoon of Curry Powder + 1/4 tablespoon of Poultry Spice + 1 teaspoon of Smoked Paprika

For the filling:

  • 5 tablespoons of Oil
  • 900 grams of Chicken Breasts, diced into tiny pieces
  • 2 medium Onions, chopped into small squares
  • 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 medium Carrot, shredded
  • 1 medium Bell Pepper, diced into tiny squares
  • 1 small can of Sweet Corn
  • 1 pack of fresh Mushroom, chopped
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of Turmeric Powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Mixed Spice
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of Mexican Spice
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of Curry Powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Poultry Spice
  • 1 teaspoon of Paprika
  • 2 teaspoons of Salt
  • 1 teaspoon of Black Pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of Mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of French Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of Spreadable Cream Cheese

Preparation Method of the Bread:

Step 1: First combine either the bread crumbs or flour with the spices and set aside. Then, combine the eggs with the spices and set aside.

Step 2: Using a rolling pin, roll over the bread until it is almost 3 milimeter thick and then, using a round cutter, cut out the slice.

Step 3: Take the round bread, dip it into the egg mixture and make sure it is covered by the mixture from both sides.

Step 4: Finally, cover it with the bread crumbs or flour and set.

Step 5: Then set aside in the freezer until filling is done.

Step 6: When filling is done, heat oil for deep fry. Reduce the heat under the oil to low-medium heat and fry the bread until golden brown and puffed.

Step 7: Allow it to cool in a sieve or over kitchen towel, then cut out half of the edge and fill it witht the filling made and serve.

Preparation Method of the Filling:

Step 8: In a pan, heat the oil and add in the onions.

Step 9: When onion is translucent, add in the garlic.

Step 10: When garlic odour is sensed, add in the chicken breast and stir.

Step 11: When chicken pieces are 3/4 cooked, add the spices and stir gently.

Step 12: Then, add in the vegetables and mix until vegetables are equally spread. Let it for 7 minutes.

Step 13: Finally, add in the sauces. Gently stir and let it all simmer for 3 minutes.

Step 14: Let it cool, and then fill the pockets.
