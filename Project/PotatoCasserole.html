Potato Casserole

This is the most creamy and light potato casserole that you'll ever try in your life.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Layer:

  • 100 grams of Butter
  • 700 grams of Chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 medium Onions, French cut
  • 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • Red and Yellow Bell Pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of Turmeric Powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of Garam Masala
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of Coriander Seed Powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of Curry Powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Poultry Spice
  • 1 teaspoon of Shawarma Spice
  • 2 teaspoons of Salt
  • 3 tablespoons of Tomato Paste

For the Cream Layer:

  • 400 milliliters of Cooking Cream
  • 2 tablespoons of Spreadable Cream Cheese
  • 3 tablespoons of Thick Cream

For the Potato Layer:

  • 4 medium Potatoes
  • 20 grams of Spreadable Cheese
  • 30 grams of butter, softened to room temperature

Preparation Method of the Chicken Layer:

Step 1: In a pan, over a medium heat melt the butter.

Step 2: Then add in the onion and saute.

Step 3: When Onion is translucent, add in the Garlic and saute

Step 4: When Garlic odour is sensed, add in the Chicken and stir.

Step 5: When Chicken is 3/4 cooked, add in the spices and stir.

Step 6: Then, add the bell peppers and stir.

Step 7: Finally, stir in the Tomato paste.

Preparation Method of the Cream Layer:

Step 8: Whisk together Cooking Cream, Spreadable Cream Cheese and Thick Cream until it is smooth.

Preparation Method of the Potato Layer:

Step 9: Boil the Potatoes until they are fully cooked.

Step 10: When boiled, allow it to cool. Then, peel off its peel and mash it using either Potato Ricer or Potato Masher.

Step 11: Add the Spreadable Cheese and butter and combine them thoroughly.

To Assemble the Potato Casserole:

Step 12: Preheat the top of the oven to 170°C.

Step 13: In a Oven Safe pyrex, add the Chicken layer.

Step 14: Then, roll the mashed potatoes in small balls or spread the potato all over the Chicken Layer.

Step 15: Add the Cream mixture and sprinkle the Mozzarella Cheese over it.

Step 16: Place the pyrex in the middle lining of the oven until the Mozzarella Cheese turns golden brown.

Bon Appétit!
