Planting Petunia Flowers

Petunia flowers are a genus of South American origins with 20 different species.

Tips on planting Petunia:

  • You need to have a sunny area as Petunia flowers bloom much much more in sun then when it is planted in partial shaded areas.
  • You don't need to water them quite often.

The most common diseases(pests) that might affect Petunia FLowers:

  • Slugs
  • Leaf Miners
  • Aphids

To avoid getting your Petunias in the risk of catching any pests, avoid watering the foliage and leafs.
