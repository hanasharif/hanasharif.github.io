Pink Pasta

Pink Pasta is a very well known dish with many recipes all around, but I gaurantee you that this is the most delicious recipe that you can ever find.

Ingredients

For boiling the Pasta:

  • 1 and 1/2 cup of Fusilli and Penne Pasta
  • 4 cups of Water
  • 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon of Italian Seasoning

For the Pink Sauce:

  • 5 tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 medium Onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 5 Baby Corns, sliced
  • 1 pack of fresh Mushroom, sliced
  • 5 Jumbo Sausages, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of Italian Seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of Curry Powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon of dried Rosemary
  • 1/2 tablespoon of dried Oregano
  • 3 tablespoons of Tomato Paste
  • 1 and 1/2 cup of Cooking Cream
  • 1 cup of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 cup of Mozzarella Cheese

Preparation Method of the Pasta:

Step 1: In a pan, bring water to boil.

Step 2: Add the Olive Oil.

Step 3: Add the Italian Seasoning.

Step 4: Add the Pasta and let it cook until al dente.

Step 5: Drain the pasta and set aside until sauce is ready.

Preparation Method of the Sauce:

Step 6: First, heat the Olive Oil in a sauce pan.

Step 7: Add the Onion and stir until translucent.

Step 8: When translucent, add in the garlic and saute.

Step 9: When garlic odour is sensed, add in the sausages and stir.

Step 10: Then, add in the spices and mix well.

Step 11: When spice odour's are sensed, add the vegetables and mix until well combined.

Step 12: Then, add the Tomato Paste and fry the paste for 1 minute.

Step 13: Add the Cooking Cream and stir well making sure that the colour of the sauce is as desired. If not, then add more tomato paste as required.

Step 14: Just as soon as the cream heats up, add the Cheddar and Mozzarella Cheese.

Step 15: When the cheeses melt, add in the drained pasta.
